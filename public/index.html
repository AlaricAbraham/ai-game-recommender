Instructions:

  • Click the "Queue" button to view games in your queue
  • Click the "Profile" button to view games in your play history
  • Click the "Discover" button to view game recommendations based on your play history and to add games to your queue/profile.
  • Use the search bar to search for specific games or describe what you want to play. You can type anything you think of!
  • Use the filter buttons to narrow down your search by game genre.
  • Click on a game card to view more details and add it to your queue.
  • Click on a game card in the queue and profie sections for more actions

While the simple AI recommendations based on your play history can be enough, you can take it a step further. Here are some examples of what's possible:

  • "I want games like Skyrim or The Witcher 3, but with a focus on action-adventure"
  • "closest thing to the movie "Jurassic Park"
  • "A game that feels like a Christopher Nolan movie"
  • "Please recommend games without considering my play history"
  • "I'm in the mood for something dark and atmospheric"
  • "Something from genres I haven't tried yet"
